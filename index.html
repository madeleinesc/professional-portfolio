About Me

I'm Madeleine and I am a full stack web developer & coder.

I spent most of my twenties travelling around the world gaining new experiences and learning all about different cultures. While living in the USA and UK, I felt that I was living out my dreams!

In 2020 - like many others - I found myself in a never-ending lockdown while living alone in Manchester. With a lot of time on my hands, I finally decided to teach myself Python which ignited a passion in me for coding. After moving back to Australia, I enrolled in a coding bootcamp to gain more experience and practice. So far, I have worked with Python (Pycharm/Jupyter Notebook), C++ (RStudio), HTML, CSS (multiple frameworks) and Javascript.

While I am still relatively new, I am enthusiastic and learn fast! If you are interested in hiring me for a project, please click the link below for my resume.
